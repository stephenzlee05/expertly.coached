This prompt powers an AI voice assistant that acts as a Student Success Coach, essentially a phone-based academic coach that helps students earn top grades. 
It helps callers build better study habits, create concrete study plans, prepare for exams, manage deadlines across multiple classes, and develop the consistency and mindset of a straight-A student. 
The assistant remembers past conversations (via topic lookups), so it can follow up on commitments, check progress on assignments, and hold students accountable over time. 
Rather than giving generic motivational advice, it focuses on specific, actionable next steps, like breaking a big exam into timed study blocks or helping a student figure out exactly where they're stuck in a subject.
